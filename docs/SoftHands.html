Soft Hands

Soft Hands causes players who bunch or hit anything with their bare hands to take damage. This feature is meant for players to think more carefully about what they bunch.


Gloves and weapons negate this effect.


To enable the Soft Hands feature:


<set xpath="/blocks/block[@name='ConfigFeatureBlock']/property[@class='AdvancedPlayerFeatures']/property[@name='SoftHands']/@value">true</set>
