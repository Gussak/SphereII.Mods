Other Mods

Introduction ››

Other Mods are available at the main github hosting site.


Bloom's Family Farming

       - This modlet turns on features in the 0-SCore related to farming, including water requirements for plants, and working pipes

       - Requires 0-SCore


CompoPackTweaks

       - This standalone modlet disables a WRN in the log files when using custom traders. It is specifically built for CompoPack and not meant to be used directly by players


Sample Project

       - This standalone modlet contains some sample code and a small buildable project for modders to learn from.


SphereII A Better Life

       - This modlet adds fish into the water ways, and new plant life.

       - Requires 0-SCore


SphereII A Round World

- A small modlet that turns on various features of 0-SCore to provide a light weight modded play

- Requires 0-SCore.


SphereII Challenges

- A small modlet that adds a new page of Challenges for players to use.

- Requires 0-SCore

SphereII Disable Sway

- A small standalone modlet that disables head bob and weapon sway for those sensitive to motion sickness

SphereII Larger Parties

- A small modlet that increases the maxium size of a party to 100, over the standard 8.


SphereII Legacy Distant Terrain

- A modlet that brings forward terrain from A17, and disables the Splat Map System

- This can be used as a small performance boost, and for people who want to add custom biomes.


SphereII Peace Of Mind

- A modlet that removes some of the less desirable scenes for mental health reasons

- Changes Party Girl's model to Marlene, mutes Trader Rekt, removes nooses, and removes the blocks with bodies handing off them.

- If 0-SCore is available, it will remove any flickering lights.


SphereII Take And Replace

- A modlet that allows you to pick up certain blocks, such as pulling boards off windows.
