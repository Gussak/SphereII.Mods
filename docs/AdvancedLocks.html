Advanced Locks

Features and Functionalities ››

In partnership with xyth, a lock picking mini-game was added to 0-SCore.


Note: You need the Locks modlet for the model:  https://github.com/7D2D/V1.0-Mods/blob/main/Locks.zip


Difficulty of the Locks is determined by the health of the locked storage unit itself. A locked storage box with more health is more difficult to pick open.


Likewise, if a locked storage container is damaged, it becomes easier to pick open.


The lock may be turned right of left using the keyboard short cuts, and holding space to try to open it.
