Software Overview

0-SCore is a modding library that was built for the 7 Days To Die modding community to enhance and add to the game's vanilla functionality.


Built over several years, and is still under active development, it's goal is to expand functionality and features.


Working with modders, we generate solutions which will meet their needs, and freely provide it to the community to use as they want.


This mod is not EAC Safe. 


This mod needs to be installed on Clients and Servers to work.


0-SCore is hosted on Github, https://github.com/SphereII/SphereII.Mods, along with other mods.


Regular releases can be found here: https://github.com/SphereII/SphereII.Mods/releases/
