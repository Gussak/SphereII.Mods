Craft From Containers

Crafting from containers allows players to craft in their backpacks and in their workstations from nearby workstations.


It also allows players to repair blocks using materials from nearby workstations as well.


This feature is disabled, by default, if an enemy is nearby. This blocks both crafting and repairing from containers.


You may also enable and disable this feature based on workstations, such as only allowing certain storage containers to work with a certain workstation.


By default, the distance for the containers to workstation is about 10 blocks.
