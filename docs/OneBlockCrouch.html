One Block Crouch

One Block Crouch allows players to crouch and enter into a one block space.


To allow players to do this, enable the OneBlockCrouch feature.


<set xpath="/blocks/block[@name='ConfigFeatureBlock']/property[@class='AdvancedPlayerFeatures']/property[@name='OneBlockCrouch']/@value">true</set>
