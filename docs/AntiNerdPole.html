AntiNerdPole

Nerd Poling is where a player jumps and places a block under their feet. Players use this technique to quickly get to safe spots, and to climb buildings quickly.


To block players from doing this, enable the AntiNerdPole feature.


<set xpath="/blocks/block[@name='ConfigFeatureBlock']/property[@class='AdvancedPlayerFeatures']/property[@name='AntiNerdPole']/@value">true</set>
