MinEventActionModifyRelatedFactionsSDX

Changes the target's relationship with the specified faction, and all the specified related factions (if any), according to the relationships between the faction and the related factions.


See full documentation:  https://github.com/SphereII/SphereII.Mods/blob/master/Mods/0-SCore/Scripts/MinEvents/MinEventActionModifyRelatedFactionsSDX.cs



<!-- Adds 10 points to the player's relationship with the red team. -->

<triggered_effect trigger="onSelfPrimaryActionEnd" action="ModifyRelatedFactionsSDX, SCore" target="self" faction="redteam" value="10" />
